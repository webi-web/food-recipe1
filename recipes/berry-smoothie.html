Berry Smoothie Bowl

Breakfast Healthy Easy
10 mins
Easy

Description

A vibrant mix of fresh berries, banana, and yogurt, topped with crunchy granola and coconut for a refreshing and nutritious start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tbsp shredded coconut
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine strawberries, blueberries, banana, and Greek yogurt. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour smoothie into a bowl.
  3. Top with granola, shredded coconut, and drizzle honey or maple syrup if desired.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

Calories220kcal
Carbs35g
Protein10g
Fat4g

Tips

  • Use frozen berries if fresh are not available for a thicker texture.
  • Adjust sweetness by adding honey or maple syrup to your taste.
  • Customize toppings with nuts, seeds, or fresh fruit slices.
