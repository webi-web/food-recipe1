Creamy Tomato Soup

Lunch Soups Easy
35 mins
Easy

Description

Rich and velvety tomato soup with a hint of basil and cream, perfect for cozy lunches or pairing with grilled cheese sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: swirl of cream for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Or carefully transfer in batches to a blender.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and chopped basil. Heat through but do not boil.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with an optional swirl of cream.

Nutrition Facts

Calories220kcal
Carbs18g
Protein4g
Fat14g

Tips

  • Use fresh basil for the best flavor.
  • Blend the soup carefully to avoid spills.
  • Pair with crusty bread or grilled cheese for a hearty meal.
